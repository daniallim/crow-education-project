# üè´ Crow Education

**Crow Education** is an open and inclusive digital learning platform designed to democratize quality education in Malaysia. Built to support **SPM students**, our mission is to bridge educational gaps by providing a free, technology-driven, and engaging learning ecosystem.

> **Hackathon Project:** This platform was developed to address the critical issue of unequal access to educational resources, ensuring that financial barriers do not hinder academic success.

---

## üéØ Project Overview

Crow Education leverages technology to transform traditional learning into an accessible, interactive digital environment.
* **For Students:** Access to high-quality learning modules and quizzes at zero cost.
* **For Teachers:** Digital tools to manage content, track progress, and enhance engagement.
* **For Communities:** A sustainable ecosystem promoting self-paced and collaborative learning.

---

## üß† Core Objectives

1.  **Universal Accessibility:** Guaranteeing access to academic materials for students of all socioeconomic backgrounds.
2.  **Digital Integration:** Empowering educators with modern tools to improve classroom outcomes.
3.  **Interactive Engagement:** Simplifying complex SPM subjects through visual aids, gamification, and simulations.
4.  **Zero-Cost Model:** Maintaining a transparent, free-to-use platform with no hidden fees.
